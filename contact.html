Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are highly recommended, especially for dinner service and weekends. Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability.

What is the dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. While jackets are not required, we ask guests to avoid athletic wear and flip-flops.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We cater to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-related requirements. Please inform us when booking.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We have private dining rooms available for events ranging from 10 to 50 guests. Contact us for more details and custom menus.

Is there a cancellation policy?

We request 24 hours notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, we require 48 hours notice.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Gift cards are available in any denomination and can be purchased online or in person. They make perfect gifts for food lovers.
